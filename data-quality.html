Assumptions made in this build.
  • The ent in each filename denotes a separate CMMS database; records are unified by client_name (one database may hold many clients).
  • Work-order cost is aggregated from the -actuals export (labor + vendor line items); asset_name is joined from the base work-order export by ID.
  • "Vendor-required" work (excluded from opportunity) is identified from reference-data/services-vendor-required.csv plus keyword rules for landscaping, snow, fire/life-safety, security, and purchases.
  • Vendor names equal to "Camelot" are treated as self-performance; "Vendor"/"Deposit"/"Final Invoice" are treated as unidentified placeholders.
  • Modeled recoverable gross profit applies a 35% margin assumption to the self-perform pool.
  • Location geography (address, city, state) is joined to assets on client_name; latitude/longitude, square footage, and year-built are not populated in the locations export.
