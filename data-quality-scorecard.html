How the score works. The composite is a weighted average of six checks: Cost recorded (20%), Labor valued when assigned (20%), Category populated (15%), Problem populated (15%), Vendor identified when outsourced (15%), Asset linked (15%). Metrics with no applicable work orders are skipped and the remaining weights re-normalized. Grades: A ≥90, B ≥80, C ≥70, D ≥60, F <60. Higher = better data capture by the team.
